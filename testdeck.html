2. User Management

Permission Sets Definitions

[cite_start]

The following permission sets are available for assignment[cite: 29]:

Permission Set Description Who Should Be Assigned
Email Template Builder Provides access to the lightning Email Template Builder. Any staff who need to create Email Templates.
Sales Engagement User Provides access to all Sales Engagement functionality, Inbox access, send email. All staff who would need to assign/reassign Leads to Cadences as part of their daily role.
Quick Cadence Creator Functionality for a user to create their own single-step repeatable "Cadences". Could be a feature rolled out to all Sales Engagement Users in the future.
Sales Engagement Cadence Creator Create cadences for your organization. The users responsible for defining the cadence processes for each department.
Access DKIM Keys For temporary access to "setup" and the DKIM keys location. Session activation required. Currently assigned to specific admins; requires running a flow to activate the session.

Cadence & Folder Sharing

  • Cadence Folder Sharing: "Cadences from Salesforce" has been removed from user view to reduce clutter. Investment Cadences are visible to the investments public group; [cite_start]Loan Cadences to the loan personnel public group[cite: 124].
  • Email Template Sharing: Managed by Alica and Vadim. [cite_start]Cadence Automated Emails are visible to all internal users[cite: 155].
  • Cadence Tracker Read/Write: Access has been opened up in "Loans Dept" and "Investments Dept" permission sets. [cite_start]This allows users to reassign the target in the cadence to themselves [cite: 130-133].

3. Outlook Integration

Org-Wide Email & DKIM

Users and Automations can use Organization-Wide Email Addresses to send messages from generic emails. [cite_start]Configure this in Setup > Organization-Wide Addresses[cite: 157].

4. Cadences

Investment Cadence - Church Visit

This cadence tracks the flow from the initial call. [cite_start]It typically includes steps for "Initial Call", followed by automated emails or follow-up tasks[cite: 163].

To Do List vs. Work Queue

Work Queue: A feature of Sales Engagement for tracking Cadence tasks.

To Do List: A replacement for the Work Queue. [cite_start]It is accessible from the Sales Engagement App only and has been added to the Utility bar for the Investments and Loans App [cite: 164-165].

[cite_start]Tip: Set your Cadence working hours in "Cadence Settings" under your personal settings to ensure tasks appear in your To Do List only during your working hours[cite: 166].

5. Leads & Automations

Lead Flow Process

The current process for a "Church Visit" lead involves creating a New Lead, adding them to a Cadence, and attempting to connect. [cite_start]If interested, the Lead is converted to a Placeholder Ministry Partner, New Business Contact, or New Opportunity[cite: 168].

Active Automations

  • Update Lead Status (Investments Cadence): Updates the Lead Status as it moves through the cadence.
    • After first contact: Status sets to "Contacted".
      • [cite_start]
    • After first automated email (2 weeks): Status sets to "Nurturing" [cite: 170-171].
    • [cite_start]
  • Remove Lead From Cadence: When a lead's status is set to "Unqualified", they are automatically removed from the Cadence[cite: 172].
    • [cite_start]
  • Lead Conversion: A flow checks for isConverted = true on the Lead object and updates the corresponding Opportunity fields[cite: 172].
